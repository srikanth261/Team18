What's in your water?

The latest survey of unregulated contaminants in U.S. tap water is the EPA third Unregulated Contaminant Monitoring Rule. All public water supplies serving more than 10,000 people and 800 representative PWSs serving 10,000 or fewer people monitored for 28 chemical contaminants from 6 groups during a 12-month period from January 2013 through December 2015.
